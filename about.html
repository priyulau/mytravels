About Me

Hi, I'm Priscilla!

I'm a Pacific Northwest townie who left the West Coast for the first time in 2012 to go to college on the East Coast. I got and used my passport for the first time after graduating college in 2016 and I haven't looked back since!

While the COVID-19 pandemic certainly put all travel to a hault, I'm determined to get caught up!

I hope you'll follow me along on my adventures, pick up some tips and tricks, and get inspired along the way.
